Get started selling like a PRO!

Step 1: Set up Evosus Retail Pro

  1. Purchase your Evosus Retail Pro license at EvosusPro.com.

Step 2: Connect your Quickbooks Online Plus account

Connect Evosus Retail Pro to your Quickbooks Online Plus account.

  • Don't have a Quickbooks Online Plus account? You can sign up here. You're eligible for a FREE 30 day trial and discounted pricing through our partnership with Intuit.
  • What if you have Quickbooks Desktop? Intuit has detailed instructions on how to import your data into QuickBooks Online, but not all of your data will be imported.

Step 3: Sign up for merchant account

Sign up for credit card processing through Evosus Payment Processing and select a credit card reader.
