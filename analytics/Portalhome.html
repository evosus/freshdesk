Our Most Popular Reports - Now Available On Your Phone or Tablet

Manage your business anytime, anywhere with Evosus Analytics. Evosus Software customers can view real-time sales data on any tablet or smartphone (iOS or Android). Evosus Analytics is based on our most popular report - The Money Tree. View year over year sales data by product line, sub department, store or employee then break down your data by percentage, quantity or dollar amount.

How do you start using Evosus Analytics?

  • doneStep 1: Verify that you meet the minimum requirements
    • Evosus Analytics requires an internet connection.
    • You must be using Evosus Business Management Software version 6.4.62 or greater. Select Help > About in Evosus Business Management Software to see what version you are using.
  • shopping_cartStep 2: Buy an Evosus Analytics license

    Please note that you'll need to purchase one license for each Evosus Analytics user.

    Once we process your purchase, you will receive an email that will walk you through the Evosus Analytics setup process, including how to log in.

  • play_for_workStep 3: Download Evosus Analytics

    Download Evosus Analytics onto your device.

    Get it on Google Play Download on the App Store
  • system_update_altStep 4: (24 hours after purchase) Fetch the Evosus Analytics license

    24 hours after purchasing your license, log into Evosus Business Management Software as an admin user, and select Help > Company Licenses > Open the Support tab > Click on the Fetch Licenses button.

  • lockStep 5: Set up permissions

    Only users that can access the Money Tree in the Evosus Business Management Software can use Evosus Analytics. You can give users access to the Money Tree using either group or user level permissions.

    • Give a group of people permission - In the Evosus Business Management Software, go to Administration > System > Security > Group Level Permissions. Select the group, open the Permissions tab, and select the Report- Money Tree permission in the Admin-Reports security category.
    • Give a single user permission - Go to Administration > System > Security > Employee Level Permissions, and then open the employee, and select the Report- Money Tree permission in the Admin-Reports security category.
  • emailStep 6: You will receive an email

    About 48 hours after purching your license, you will receive an email notifying you that your account is set up. Once you get this email, you're ready to use Evosus Analytics.

  • open_in_browserStep 7: Use a compatible browser to access Evosus Analytics

    Evosus Analytics supports the following browsers.

    • Chrome version 49 or greater (and Chrome for Android version 51 or greater)
    • Firefox version 47 or greater (and the most recent stable version of Firefox for Android)
    • Safari version 9.1 or greater (and Safari for iOS version 9.3 or greater)
    • Edge version 13 or greater
